## Overview  

ShutterCount for macOS is a detailed and reliable camera actuation monitor that helps photographers keep track of their device’s true usage. It reads the exact shutter count from the camera’s memory, providing insight into the total number of photos taken and the overall wear on the camera’s mechanics.  

Tailored for Canon cameras, the app connects seamlessly via USB and displays the data in a clean, professional interface. ShutterCount is perfect for photographers buying or selling used gear, allowing them to verify the actual lifespan of their camera in seconds.  

With full support for modern macOS systems and both Intel and Apple Silicon architectures, ShutterCount offers smooth performance and precise readings. Users can also track additional camera statistics, including live view duration and power-on cycles, ensuring full transparency of their device’s history.  

Whether you are a professional photographer or a hobbyist, ShutterCount provides a dependable and easy way to access accurate shutter count information — a must-have for anyone managing multiple cameras or verifying camera health.
